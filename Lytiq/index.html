STRATEGY CONSULTING DIGITALIZATION & AI

Idea generation and strategy workshops based on the possibilities and (current) limits of artificial intelligence and analytics. We help you not only to identify new ways to improve your business processes, but also to evaluate them in terms of feasibility, effort and benefit. On request, we can also support you in setting up your KI & Analytics competence center.

IMPLEMENTATION OF AI

We are your implementation partner for projects in the field of AI, data science and digitization. We not only deliver detached algorithms, but also create a specific solution for you “from end to end” after analyzing your special situation: We start with the raw data that a production control system returns, camera images, audio recordings or internal company data silos. We use this data for our analyzes and to find a solution to your problem with our algorithms. We then integrate the resulting software into your business processes, controls, analyzes and dashboards.
